Frequently Asked Questions

How does the prediction algorithm work?

Our prediction algorithm uses a combination of machine learning techniques and medical data to analyze various factors such as age, BMI, blood pressure, and other relevant health indicators to determine the risk of developing diabetes.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is protected with industry-standard encryption and is kept confidential. We prioritize the security and privacy of our users' information.

Can I trust the predictions?

Our algorithm is built on well-researched medical data and is designed to provide accurate and reliable predictions. However, it is always recommended to consult with a healthcare provider for a professional diagnosis.

How often should I use the prediction tool?

It is recommended to use the prediction tool regularly, especially if there are changes in your health status or lifestyle. Regular monitoring can help in early detection and better management of diabetes risk.
